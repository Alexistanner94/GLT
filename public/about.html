About GLTA

What is GTLA?

Golf League Tournament Action is an app that administers a fantasy golf pool over the course of a season.

How to start?

Sign Up or Log In to compile a team. Participants select a team of 5 golfers at the beginning of the season.

How to win!?

The team whose golfers recieve the most prize money at the end of the season wins!

Our Story

Golf League Tournament Action is a brainchild of Graeme Stewart, CEO of Blue Team. Traditionally playing in a fantasy golf pool with some his ole' pals, they would have to mail in (pen and paper type mail) their picks to the commisioner. This commisioner would then compile tournament results and golfer payouts by hand, over the course of a season. Being a modernized Scot & American citizen, he sought to bring about a quicker and digital version of this golf pool into existence. With the founding of Blue Team, his dream has become a reality.
